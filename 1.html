About Our School

Sri Guru Harkrishan Public School came into being in 1992 with the mission of providing value-oriented education while inspiring and kindling the quest for knowledge. The foundation stone of the school was laid by the former Chief Minister of Punjab, Late S. Beant Singh.

The school upholds the ideals of truthfulness, compassion, peace, discipline, dynamic thinking, and ethical values, inspired by the teachings of Sri Guru Harkrishan Ji, the most brilliant prodigy in the world. The school received affiliation from the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) in 1995 up to the Senior Secondary level (Affiliation No. 1630112). Since then, it has continued to nurture students into responsible citizens and future leaders of the nation.

The institution functions under the presidentship of S. Jagjit Singh Dardi, who is also the Chairman of Chardikala Time TV and Chardikala Daily Newspaper. The school is directed by Mrs. Jaswinder Kaur Dardi.

Sri Guru Harkrishan Public Management Society

Sri Guru Harkrishan Public School is one of the leading institutions managed by the Sri Guru Harkrishan Public Management Society. The school is located at Shanti Nagar, Tafazalpura, Near Urban Estate Phase-II.

The institution provides quality education up to the Senior Secondary level in all major streams:

  • Medical
  • Non-Medical
  • Commerce
  • Arts

School Curriculum

The curriculum is designed to maintain a perfect balance between academic excellence and co-curricular activities. Special emphasis is placed on developing moral values, leadership, discipline, creativity, and confidence.

The school believes that every child is unique and possesses immense potential. Various educational and extracurricular activities help students discover and develop their hidden talents.

Under the dynamic leadership of Principal Dr. Kanwaljit Kaur, students have consistently excelled in academics and sports, winning numerous medals and awards at the District, State, and National levels.
